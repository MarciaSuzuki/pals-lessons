LESSON 2 · STATION 1 · SILENT PHASE
You, Me, Us — Pronouns
Pointing is teaching.
In this lesson, a friend shows you what words like I, you, and they mean — just by pointing. Watch carefully. Listen. There is nothing to say.
Eight words, four pairs
You'll meet them two at a time, with practice after each pair.
I and you — he and she — we and they — this and that.
Listening

Listen.
Well done.
Eight pointing words — the hinges of every conversation. They'll come back often as the lessons go on. The more you meet them, the deeper they settle.
What you met
I, you — the two people talking.
he, she — a third person, one at a time.
we, they — groups, including or not including you.
this, that — the near thing and the far thing.
